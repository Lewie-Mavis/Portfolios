Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Learn how we protect your data across Africa.

Last Updated: March 2026

Effective for all African jurisdictions

1. Introduction

Summit Media Africa is committed to protecting your privacy and handling personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws across Africa, including:

  • Kenya's Data Protection Act (2019)
  • Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR)
  • South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA)
  • Ghana's Data Protection Act (2012)

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you engage with our Services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process:

Personal Details

Name, job title, company, email address, and phone number.

Business Information

Campaign objectives, budgets, and marketing data.

Technical Data

Cookies, browser type, IP address, and engagement analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Deliver and manage media campaigns effectively
  • Provide analytics, reports, and ROI insights
  • Communicate with clients about projects and updates
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, and tax obligations

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or rent personal data.

We may share data only with:

  • Media partners and broadcasters for campaign delivery
  • Analytics providers and technology partners who process data on our behalf
  • Legal or regulatory authorities when required by law

All third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it solely for the agreed purpose.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security controls — including encryption, access restrictions, and secure servers — to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Encryption Access Control Secure Servers 24/7 Monitoring

6. Cross-Border Data Transfers

As a Pan-African company, your data may be processed in multiple jurisdictions. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable cross-border data transfer regulations and maintain equivalent protection standards in every country of operation.

Kenya Uganda Tanzania Rwanda Ghana Nigeria South Africa

7. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

Access your personal data
Correct your personal data
Delete your personal data
Withdraw consent for data processing
Object to direct marketing
Lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority

Requests may be sent to privacy@summitmediaafrica.com

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience and analyze traffic. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our site

Marketing Cookies

Used to deliver relevant advertisements

9. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect legal changes or improvements in our practices. The latest version will always be posted on our website.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any changes.
