About Us

We are a dedicated association empowering young anglophone African professionals in Europe. Through mentorship, networking, and collaboration, we bridge the gap between emerging talent and diversity-driven companies.

Our mission is to create a supportive community that fosters professional growth, inclusivity, and meaningful connections, inspiring each member to achieve their career aspirations.

Get In Touch

Our Story

Having lived and worked in France for over five years, we understand the complexities of transitioning into a new linguistic and cultural professional landscape.

We quickly noticed that we were facing the same challenges in our professional journeys: integration, finding professional opportunities, advocating for better remuneration, etc.

We aim to harness our diverse experiences and synthesize them into a supportive framework for young African professionals.

Our Values

Our core values guide us in all our endeavors, ensuring we stay committed to our mission of empowering young African professionals.

Empowerment

We Focus on equipping members with the skills, knowledge, and resources necessary to advance their careers. This value highlights the importance of mentorship, training, and support in fostering individual and collective growth.

Collaboration

We encourage a spirit of cooperation and networking among members to foster mutual success. We recognize that collective efforts lead to greater opportunities and achievements.

Cultural Synergy

GACC celebrates the fusion of African and global cultures, recognizing the unique perspectives and strengths that arise from this intersection. We champion the idea that embracing cultural diversity enhances professional and personal growth.

Global Connectivity

We recognize the importance of a global perspective in professional development. We encourage our members to leverage their unique backgrounds and experiences to engage with global networks, creating opportunities that extend beyond local boundaries.

Sustainable Development

We are dedicated to fostering workplace equality, providing diverse career opportunities, and promoting sustainable practices that empower individuals and communities across the globe.

Diverse Career Opportunities

We purpose to promote decent work conditions ( SDG 8 ) by connecting members to diverse career opportunities.

Workplace Equality

We aim to reduce inequalities ( SDG 10 ) by advocating for workplace inclusion and equal access.

Global Collaborations

We work to foster global collaboration ( SDG 17 ) through partnerships with diversity- driven companies and institutions.

Why Partner With Us?

Discover the numerous benefits of partnering with GACC. Join us in empowering young African professionals and enhancing your brand’s reach while gaining access to valuable resources and networks.

Brand Visibility

Partners enjoy visibility at GACC events, on digital platforms, and in promotional materials. Publicly supporting GACC helps build your brand’s reputation as a champion of diversity, appealing to customers and clients who value social responsibility.

Access to a Diverse Talent Database

Through exclusive access to GACC’s talent pool and events, partners can meet, interview, and onboard qualified candidates who bring unique perspectives and experiences.

Access to New Market Insights and Global Networks

African professionals offer valuable insights into emerging markets in Africa, benefiting companies interested in expanding or enhancing services in these regions.

Our Partners

We are proud to work with leading organizations that share our commitment to excellence. These partnerships help us drive innovation, deliver better services, and achieve mutual growth.

A leading provider of recruitment services focused on helping companies find diverse talent, ensuring a more inclusive and equitable workforce for global markets.

An initiative aimed at elevating African women into leadership positions across various industries, fostering a more inclusive and dynamic workforce for the future.
