Meet the Leadership

The experienced team driving Tasvanna's mission to connect global businesses with exceptional Kenyan talent

DW

Douglas Wainaina

Founder & Managing Partner

Nairobi, Kenya

"I didn't build Tasvanna from a boardroom. I built it from the inside as someone who did the work, saw the talent, and watched great people go underpaid and overlooked simply because the right doors weren't open to them."

Before founding Tasvanna, Douglas worked as an outsourced professional for an international company, moving across roles from customer support and document validation to data analysis, cleaning, and quality assurance. He didn't just manage talent. He was the talent.

That experience gave him something most outsourcing founders don't have: a firsthand understanding of what quality delivery actually looks like, and the gap that exists for skilled African professionals trying to access global opportunities, not because the talent wasn't there, but because the structure, access, and compensation weren't.

Today, Douglas builds the bridge he wished existed, connecting early-stage tech startups in the US and beyond with structured, reliable African talent across customer experience, data operations, and back-office functions. He works directly alongside his team on every engagement, ensuring clients get both the relationship and the results.

Delivered in half the time

A recent client needed a project completed in two months. Tasvanna delivered in one, with quality signed off directly by the client. That's the standard Douglas holds every engagement to.
