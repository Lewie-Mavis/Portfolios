African Legends Publishing

African Legends Publishing is more than a publishing house—we are a movement to preserve African identity through the power of storytelling. We partner with visionaries, changemakers, and everyday heroes across the continent to publish stories that matter. From thought-provoking memoirs to cultural anthologies, our mission is to ensure that every African voice has a place on the global bookshelf.

Through tailored publishing experiences, editorial support, and global distribution, we empower authors to share their journeys, wisdom, and legacy with the world.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be a writer to get published?

No. We guide you through interviews and ghostwriting—your story, our words.

Can I publish my book internationally?

Yes. Your book will be listed on Amazon and printed locally across regions.

How long does the publishing process take?

On average, it takes about 90 days from story development to final print.

Will I own the rights to my book?

Yes. You retain full ownership and copyright of your published work.

Can I request book design or cover changes?

Absolutely. Our team works closely with you to ensure the final design reflects your vision.
